Our Story

A Family Tradition

At Penguin Fish and Chips, we take pride in serving freshly made fish and chips, cooked to crispy perfection in 100% vegetable oil. Our fish is hand-battered and fried on demand, ensuring every bite is hot, light, and flavorful.


More than just a meal, we offer a warm and welcoming experience for our community. Whether you’re grabbing a quick bite or enjoying a meal with family, we’re here to serve up delicious, freshly prepared food that keeps our customers coming back. Stop by and taste the difference!
